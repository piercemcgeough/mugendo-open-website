Knocknarea Arena ATU (formally Sligo IT)

Dear sporting friends

Mugendo Association of Ireland would like to invite you to the Mugendo Open 2023 which will take place on Saturday 11th Feb in our competition home of Sligo in the Knocknarea Arena ATU (formally Sligo IT).

The Mugendo Open will run under WAKO kickboxing rules but entries are open to all styles and associations and we would encourage competitors to travel so that can give everyone a different experience.

We wish to make the best and most memorable Mugendo Open to date. We also want to thank you for your continued support in the past and into the future.

Yours in sport,
Mugendo Association of Ireland
